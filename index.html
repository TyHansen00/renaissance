about the project

renaissance is an art installation we are creating for cs267, a course at colby college. the audience will appraoch a blank canvas and a photo is taken of them. a CAN (creative adversarial network) will create an original work of art, make it the background of the canvas, and then a neural style transfer will be conducted to "paint" the user into the work. every piece is unique, so you will never get the same picture as someone else.
